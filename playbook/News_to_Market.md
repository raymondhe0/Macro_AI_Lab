# 📘 The News-to-Market Playbook

## 🎯 Motivation
This playbook is designed to help investment learners build a systematic thinking framework from **"reading the news"** to **"foreseeing market impacts."** By transforming fragmented current events into structured logical deductions, investors can:
* Distinguish market **noise** from **signals**.
* Understand the **inter-market relationships** between assets.
* Develop the ability to independently analyze macroeconomic and industry trends.

---

## 🛠 Usage Guidelines
1. **Daily Scan:** Observe abnormal fluctuations in **US Treasury yields**, **Crude Oil**, and **Index Futures**.
2. **Select Headlines:** Pick 3 major events involving macroeconomics, central banks, or geopolitics from core sources (e.g., Bloomberg).
3. **Logical Deduction:** Enforce the analysis path: `Event` → `Direct Impact` → `Secondary Transmission` → `Asset Correlation`.
4. **Benchmark Views:** Compare your deductions with professional analysts (e.g., Bloomberg Opinion) to correct cognitive biases.

---

## 📑 Daily Review Template (Example Case: March 2026)

### 1. Core Event
**What happened?** *Example:* Brent Oil surged past **$100/barrel** after the Houthis joined the Iran conflict, threatening major global shipping lanes in the Red Sea.

### 2. Logic Chain
* **Primary Impact:** Immediate spike in global energy prices and freight costs. Energy sector (**XOM**, **CVX**) rallies; transportation and consumer discretionary sectors face margin pressure.
* **Secondary Transmission:** Higher oil prices trigger a spike in **Inflation (CPI)** expectations. This forces the market to anticipate a hawkish **Federal Reserve**, pushing the **10-Year Treasury Yield** higher (e.g., toward 4.5%).
* **Final Destination:** Rising yields increase the discount rate for valuations. This leads to a sharp sell-off in **Nasdaq (Tech stocks)**, as future earnings are worth less in today's dollars.

### 3. Inter-market Signals
| Instrument | Predicted Trend | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DXY (USD Index)** | 📈 Strengthening | Capital seeks **Safe-haven** status and higher US yields. |
| **10-Year Yield** | 📈 Rising | Acts as the "anchor" for the tech sell-off. |
| **VIX Index** | ⚠️ Spiking (20-25+) | Indicates a transition from "complacency" to "fear." |
